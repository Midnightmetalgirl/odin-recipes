The Best Homemade Ice Cream Recipe: Ice Cream in a Jar!

This is the best homemade recipe for ice cream I have ever come across! It has simple ingredients and is SUPER customizable! The reason why it says that it is in a jar is because you can shake it up and freeze it all in a mason jar. That means less dishes and cleanup!!! Check out the ingredients below and mix away!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • .5 cup Milk
  • Vanilla or other flavors
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 Medium Mason Jars

Mixing Instructions

  1. Add .5 milk and 1 cup heavy whipping cream evenly into the jars.
  2. Add flavoring and sugar evenly into the jars
  3. Shake the jars violently until there is barely any sound
  4. Put in freezer and let set for 2 hours
  5. Enjoy!!!
